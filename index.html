About Our College

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

K.R.K. Government Degree College, Addanki has been a beacon of quality higher education in Prakasam District since its establishment on December 20, 1984, by the Government of Andhra Pradesh. Affiliated with APSCHE, we offer free CBCS-based undergraduate programmes that blend traditional values with modern academic rigour.

🎓
APSCHE Affiliated
Recognised by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education, ensuring curriculum quality and academic standards.
🔬
State-of-the-Art Laboratories
Well-equipped Science, Computer, Botany & Zoology labs enabling hands-on learning experiences.
🏆
Outstanding Results
Consistently achieving near 100% pass rates in APSCHE examinations with multiple rank holders.
