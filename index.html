About Our College

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

K.R.K. Government Degree College, Addanki — named after the philanthropist Sri Katta Rama Koteswara Rao — was established on 20th December 1984 by the Government of Andhra Pradesh (G.O.Ms.No.509) on the scenic Singarakonda Hillocks, Bapatla District. Affiliated to Andhra Kesari University (AKU), Ongole, we proudly offer completely free quality higher education to the poor and marginalised.

🎓
AKU Affiliated
Affiliated to Andhra Kesari University (AKU), Ongole — recognised by UGC under 2(f) & 12(b). NAAC accredited with Grade A.
🎁
100% Free Education
All undergraduate courses are fully free of charge for eligible students. Jagananna Vidya Deevena & other AP scholarship schemes available.
🔬
Modern Campus & Labs
21.5-acre campus with 20 classrooms, 6 science labs, 3 computer labs, 10-acre sports ground and a well-equipped GYM.
