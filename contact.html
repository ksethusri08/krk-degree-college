Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is KRK Degree College government recognised?
Yes. KRK Degree College is fully affiliated with APSCHE (Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education) and recognised by the UGC (University Grants Commission) and AP Government Department of Higher Education.
Are government scholarships available for students?
Yes. Eligible students can avail of various AP Government scholarships including Jagananna Vidya Deevena (fee reimbursement), PRERANA, and Post-Matric scholarships for SC/ST/BC/Minority students. Our office provides full assistance with scholarship applications.
What is the medium of instruction?
The primary medium of instruction is English. Telugu is also used where necessary to aid comprehension, particularly in first year for students transitioning from Telugu medium intermediate education.
Does the college have hostel facilities?
The college does not currently operate its own hostel but can assist students in finding safe, affordable accommodation near the campus. Several approved private hostels are available close to the college.
Can students from other states / boards apply?
Yes. Students from CBSE, ICSE, and other state boards are welcome to apply. Inter-state migration certificate (eligibility certificate) from APSCHE may be required for students from outside AP.
What extracurricular activities are available?
KRK offers a wide range of activities: NCC, NSS, Cultural Club, Debate Society, Science Club, Sports teams (cricket, volleyball, athletics), Annual Day, and participation in inter-college competitions.
