About KRK Degree College

A legacy of academic excellence, values, and student-centred education in the heart of Andhra Pradesh.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🌟

Our Vision

To be a leading centre of excellence in higher education in Andhra Pradesh, nurturing intellectually capable, morally upright, and socially responsible graduates who contribute meaningfully to society and the nation.

🎯

Our Mission

  • Provide quality undergraduate education aligned with APSCHE standards and CBCS framework
  • Foster critical thinking, research aptitude, and problem-solving skills
  • Promote holistic development through sports, culture, NCC, and community service
  • Create an inclusive environment welcoming students from all backgrounds
  • Build industry-relevant skills to ensure graduate employability
  • Uphold integrity, discipline, and the pursuit of knowledge

Our Journey

A Legacy Built Over Three Decades

From a modest beginning to a thriving institution of higher learning, KRK Degree College's history is a story of dedication, growth, and unwavering commitment to student excellence.

1995
Foundation & Establishment
KRK Degree College was founded with a vision to provide accessible, quality higher education to the students of Andhra Pradesh. Started with BSc programme.
2000
APSCHE Affiliation Secured
Officially affiliated with the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education, validating our academic standards and curriculum.
2005
BZC & BCom Programmes Launched
Expanded our academic offerings with Life Sciences (BZC) and Commerce with Computers (BCom) departments, doubling student intake.
2012
New Campus Wing & Computer Lab
State-of-the-art computer laboratory with 60 systems and high-speed internet inaugurated. Library expanded to 10,000+ volumes.
2019
CBCS Framework Adoption
Successfully transitioned to Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) as mandated by APSCHE, enhancing curriculum flexibility and student choice.
2024
Digital Transformation & Smart Classrooms
Introduced smart classrooms, digital learning platforms, and enhanced placement cell activities to meet modern industry demands.
