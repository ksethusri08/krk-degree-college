About KRK Govt. Degree College

Serving Addanki & Bapatla District since 20th December 1984 — a Government institution of free, inclusive education on the Singarakonda Hillocks. "THAMASOMA JYOTHIRGAMAYA"

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

✨

Our Vision

"THAMASOMA JYOTHIRGAMAYA"

Lead us from Darkness to Light — may the Light of Knowledge remove the darkness of Ignorance from the minds of young men and women by providing quality higher education.

To be a leading Government institution of higher learning in Bapatla District, nurturing intellectually capable, morally upright and socially responsible graduates who serve their communities and the nation.

🎯

Our Mission

  • Enrich curriculum with skills, values & knowledge to shape competent, committed and compassionate human resources
  • Achieve enhanced learning through innovative methods, skill enhancement workshops and seminars
  • Bring the benefits of higher education to poor, marginalised and vulnerable sections who are our main stakeholders
  • Attain a better NAAC grade and improve NIRF ranking through quality improvements
  • Foster research, publications, MOUs and industry linkages for student advancement
  • Promote social concern, integrity, perseverance, transparency and leadership among students

Principal's Message

👨‍🏫
Dr. V. Mohana Rao
Principal
M.Sc. Ph.D. 30+ Yrs Exp.

"It is with immense pride that I welcome you to K.R.K. Government Degree College, Addanki — established on 20th December 1984 by the Government of Andhra Pradesh on the scenic Singarakonda Hillocks. Named after the philanthropist Sri Katta Rama Koteswara Rao, our college has been a beacon of free, quality higher education for the poor and marginalised of Bapatla District for over four decades.

Affiliated to Andhra Kesari University (AKU), Ongole, and recognised by UGC under 2(f) & 12(b), our institution upholds the motto "THAMASOMA JYOTHIRGAMAYA" — Lead us from Darkness to Light. We believe education is the greatest equaliser, and every student — regardless of their background — deserves quality learning completely free of cost. Our dedicated faculty across 14 departments and our modern 21.5-acre campus create an environment where young minds truly flourish.

I invite every aspiring student to join the KRK family. The years you spend here will shape you into not just graduates, but into capable, compassionate, and responsible citizens who will make Andhra Pradesh proud."

Our Journey

A Legacy Built Over Four Decades

From a modest beginning on the Singarakonda Hillocks — a famous pilgrim centre for Lord Prasannanjaneya Swamy — to a thriving institution of higher learning. Before 1984, students from Addanki had to travel to Ongole, Narsaraopet or Guntur for higher education. KRK Degree College changed that forever.

1984
Foundation — G.O.Ms.No.509
K.R.K. Government Degree College was established on 20th December 1984 by the Government of AP (G.O.Ms.No.509, Education Dept., Dt. 28-11-1984), named after philanthropist Sri Katta Rama Koteswara Rao, on Singarakonda Hillocks, Addanki. First Principal: Sri Jammi Koneti Rao (famous scientist who designed the college emblem & motto).
1990s
Acharya Nagarjuna University Affiliation
Formally affiliated with Acharya Nagarjuna University, establishing academic credibility and enabling the first batches of BSc, BA & BCom graduates from Addanki to pursue higher studies.
2000s
Expansion & UGC Recognition
Secured UGC recognition under 2(f) & 12(b). Expanded to 14 departments covering Science, Humanities and Commerce streams. NAAC accreditation achieved with Grade B, validating quality standards.
2010s
Infrastructure & Digital Growth
Added 3 Computer Labs, 3 e-Classrooms with smart boards, 2 Skill Development labs, and expanded the campus to 21.5 acres with a 10-acre sports ground. NSS & NCC units (2 each) established.
2022
Andhra Kesari University (AKU) Affiliation
College re-affiliated to the newly established Andhra Kesari University (AKU), Ongole — a dedicated university for Bapatla District — further strengthening academic ties and student opportunities.
2024–25
40 Years of Excellence & Counting
Celebrating 40+ years of free, inclusive education on the Singarakonda Hillocks. JKC Programme, Women Empowerment Cell, Red Ribbon Club and digital classrooms now serve 2500+ students annually.
