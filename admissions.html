Eligibility Criteria

Who Can Apply?

🔬 BSc Programme

B.Sc — Computers & Mathematics

  • 10+2 / Intermediate (any board) passed with minimum 45% aggregate
  • Must have studied Mathematics as one of the subjects
  • Preference given to MPC (Maths, Physics, Chemistry) students
  • No upper age limit; fresh & lateral admissions considered
  • SC/ST/BC/EWS reservations as per AP Government norms
🌿 BZC Programme

B.Sc — Botany, Zoology & Chemistry

  • 10+2 / Intermediate passed with minimum 45% aggregate
  • Must have Biology / Botany / Zoology as a subject
  • Preference given to BiPC (Biology, Physics, Chemistry) students
  • Medical aspirants (NEET) encouraged — strong biology base
  • SC/ST/BC/EWS reservations as per AP Government norms
📊 BA & BCom

B.A & B.Com with Computers

  • 10+2 / Intermediate passed with minimum 40% aggregate
  • BCom: Commerce background preferred but not mandatory
  • BA: Open to all streams — MPC, BiPC, CEC, HEC, MEC
  • Special consideration for first-generation college students
  • SC/ST/BC/EWS reservations as per AP Government norms
